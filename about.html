About Saraswati...

Goddess Saraswati is the Hindu goddess of learning, wisdom, music, and aesthetics. She is also known as Bharati (eloquence), Shatarupa (existence), Vedamata ('mother of the Vedas'), Brahmi, Sarada, Vagisvari, and Putkar

As Vac, she is the goddess of speech. The four hands hold items with symbolic meaning — a pustaka (book), a mālā (rosary, garland), a water pot and a musical instrument (vīnā).

The book she holds symbolizes the Vedas representing the universal, divine, eternal, and true knowledge as well as all forms of learning.
