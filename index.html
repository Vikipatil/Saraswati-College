About Saraswati College

Saraswati is the Goddess of Learning and Knowledge. By sitting on a lotus, Saraswati signifies that She is Herself rooted in the Supreme Reality, and symbolizes supreme knowledge

Goddess Saraswati is the Hindu goddess of learning, wisdom, music, and aesthetics. She is also known as Bharati (eloquence), Shatarupa (existence), Vedamata ('mother of the Vedas'), Brahmi, Sarada, Vagisvari, and Putkari. As Vac, she is the goddess of speech. The four hands hold items with symbolic meaning — a pustaka (book), a mālā (rosary, garland), a water pot and a musical instrument (vīnā).
